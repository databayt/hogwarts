### Key Features

- **Student Information System** - Comprehensive student profiles, enrollment, and academic records
- **Attendance Management** - Digital attendance tracking with leave management
- **Examination System** - Complete exam scheduling, grading, and result processing
- **Timetable Management** - Smart scheduling with conflict resolution
- **Fee Management** - Online fee collection, receipts, and outstanding tracking
- **Library System** - Digital catalog, issue/return tracking, and fine management
- **Communication Hub** - Announcements, messaging, and notifications
- **Parent Portal** - Real-time access to student progress and school updates
- **Reports & Analytics** - Comprehensive reporting for data-driven decisions
