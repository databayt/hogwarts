### 6. Chart of Accounts (5 minutes)

```
ASSETS (What school owns)
  1000 - Cash
    1010 - Operating Account
    1020 - Savings Account
    1030 - Tuition Collection
  1100 - Accounts Receivable (outstanding fees from parents)
  1200 - Inventory (books, supplies)
  1300 - Property & Equipment (buildings, furniture, computers)

LIABILITIES (What school owes)
  2000 - Accounts Payable (unpaid vendor bills)
  2100 - Salaries Payable (unpaid staff salaries)
  2200 - Loans Payable (bank loans)

EQUITY (Owner's investment)
  3000 - Retained Earnings (accumulated profits)
  3100 - Current Year Earnings (this year's profit/loss)

REVENUE (Income)
  4000 - Tuition Revenue
  4100 - Other Revenue (fundraising, events, rentals)

EXPENSES (Costs)
  5000 - Salaries & Benefits
  5100 - Utilities
  5200 - Supplies
  5300 - Maintenance
  5400 - Professional Services
```

**Double-Entry Example**:
```
Transaction: Parent pays SDG 2,400 tuition

Entry 1: Debit Cash +SDG 2,400 (asset increases)
Entry 2: Credit Accounts Receivable -SDG 2,400 (liability decreases)

Result: Cash up, Outstanding fees down (balanced)
```

**View Account Details**:
1. Click account: "1010 - Operating Account"

   - Opening balance: SDG 100,000

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Accountants
- **Daily Reconciliation**: Review bank transactions daily
- **Weekly Collections Review**: Monitor fee collection rates
- **Monthly Budget Review**: Check budget vs actual, adjust if needed
- **Quarterly Audit**: Internal audit of all accounts

### For Administrators
- **Monitor KPIs**: Check dashboard weekly (collection rate, cash balance)
- **Approve Expenses Promptly**: Don't delay reimbursements
- **Review Financial Reports**: Monthly P&L and Balance Sheet
- **Plan for Cash Flow**: Maintain 3-6 months operating expenses

### For Parents
- **Pay on Time**: Avoid late fees (5% after 30 days)
- **Use Online Payment**: Faster and more secure than cash
- **Check Wallet Balance**: Ensure student has funds for canteen
- **Download Receipts**: Keep for tax purposes

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Invoice not generating
**Solution**:
- Verify fee structure assigned to student
- Check student status (must be ACTIVE)
- Review invoice generation settings (enabled?)

### Issue: Payment not reflecting
**Solution**:
- Check payment status in Stripe/bank
- Verify webhook configuration (for online payments)
- Manual reconciliation: Match payment to invoice

### Issue: Payroll calculation incorrect
**Solution**:
- Review timesheet hours (overtime calculated correctly?)
- Check deductions (tax rate, social security)
- Verify salary structure (base + bonuses)
