## Key Metrics & Widgets

### 📊 Enrollment Statistics

Real-time student enrollment data:

- **Total Students** - Current active enrollment
- **New Admissions** - This academic year
- **Pending Applications** - Awaiting approval
- **Dropouts** - Students who left
- **Alumni** - Graduated students

### 👥 Staff Overview

Complete staff management metrics:

- **Teaching Staff** - Active teachers
- **Non-Teaching Staff** - Support personnel
- **Attendance Rate** - Staff presence
- **Leave Requests** - Pending approvals
- **Performance** - Average ratings

### 💰 Financial Summary

Financial health at a glance:

- **Fee Collection** - Monthly/yearly totals
- **Outstanding Dues** - Pending payments
- **Collection Rate** - Payment efficiency
- **Expenses** - Monthly expenditure
- **Revenue Trends** - Growth indicators

### ✅ Attendance Analytics

Comprehensive attendance tracking:

- **Today's Attendance** - Real-time percentage
- **Weekly Trends** - 7-day overview
- **Class-wise Data** - Detailed breakdown
- **Absentee List** - Students absent today
- **Leave Requests** - Pending approvals
