Hogwarts is an open-source, multi-tenant K-12 platform shipping in production at [ed.databayt.org](https://ed.databayt.org), with a live pilot at [kingfahad.databayt.org](https://kingfahad.databayt.org) (King Fahad Schools — founding member of the [MENA-10 free pilot cohort](/docs/pilot)). Where the established SIS vendors target large North-American districts with closed, English-first stacks, Hogwarts is Arabic-first, RTL-native, subdomain-isolated per school, and priced for emerging-market budgets. Browse the vendors below for inspiration; the feature comparison sets a baseline for parity and the [battle-card section](#why-mena-schools-choose-hogwarts) at the bottom is the prose for proposals and discovery calls.

## Assessment & exam-creation tools

These are not full-SIS competitors — they are point tools for **exam, test, and quiz creation**. We track them because the exams module's creation UX competes with them directly, and because they map the design space for "how a teacher builds an exam." We researched ~25; the patterns below directly informed the [3-mode "Create Exam"](/docs/exams#generation) direction (Adopt a blueprint · Generate with AI · Build from scratch).

### Design lessons

- **Fast = adopt a blueprint, or prompt an AI.** SchoolDeck/Testmate hit < 2 minutes by pre-baking the curriculum blueprint; AI tools hit 2–3 minutes from a topic prompt. The multi-step configuration wizard is the slow path teachers abandon.
- **Teachers skip configuration.** Per-question-type difficulty and Bloom distributions are almost always defaulted or skipped — design for "click through with sensible defaults," not "configure everything."
- **2–3 fixed professional layouts suffice.** Pixel-level header/footer/cover customization is Canva's job, not assessment software's; it is the over-engineering to avoid.
- **Print-ready PDF with branding set once is non-negotiable** for MENA/Sudan, where paper exams dominate.
- **A platform-curated, curriculum-aligned library is the moat.** AssessPrep's 60k IGCSE bank and SchoolDeck's baked-in CBSE blueprints are why schools pay on day one. Our exam catalog/adopt already proves this pattern.

**The gap nobody fills:** professional **Arabic-RTL exam papers** and **bilingual report cards**. No researched tool handles Arabic-primary paper formatting well — this is our regional advantage.

## Differentiators

- **Arabic-first, RTL-native** — every screen designed for Arabic first, English second.
- **Open source** — schools can audit the code; community contributes; no vendor lock-in.
- **Multi-tenant by design** — `schoolId`-scoped queries, subdomain routing, complete data isolation per school.
- **Region-priced** — free tier up to 100 students; Pro $1.50/student/month; Enterprise $1.00/student/month.
- **MEA payment rails** — Stripe + bankak + tap + cash + bank transfer + mobile money.
- **AI-assisted admission** — document classification, merit ranking, bank-receipt extraction.
