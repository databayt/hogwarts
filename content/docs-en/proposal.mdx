A proposal is the artifact that moves a school from [Trial](/docs/sales) (Stage 3) to signed pilot or paid contract. This page holds the templates. Templates are starting points — every proposal must be customized for the school name, student count, country, and decision maker. For pilot strategy see [Pilot](/docs/pilot); for tier pricing reference see [Business Model](/docs/business-model).

## When to send a proposal

| Trigger                                                    | Proposal type            |
| ---------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Discovery + Trial complete; school asks "what next?"       | Free Pilot Proposal      |
| Pilot month 5; school engaged; conversion conversation due | Paid Conversion Proposal |
| Government / district approach; multi-school RFP           | District / Government    |
| Network of schools (3+) under same owner                   | Multi-School Bundle      |

If the school has not asked for next steps, do not send. Discovery → demo → trial → ask → propose. Out of order = noise.

## Proposal anatomy

Every Hogwarts proposal contains the same eight sections, in this order:

| #   | Section         | What goes here                                                     |
| --- | --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1   | Cover           | School name, contact, date, our name + role                        |
| 2   | Understanding   | What we heard about their pain (3–5 sentences, school's own words) |
| 3   | What we propose | The pilot or paid offer in one paragraph                           |
| 4   | What's included | Feature list scoped to school size                                 |
| 5   | Timeline        | Day 0 → Day 30 → Day 90 → Day 180                                  |
| 6   | Investment      | Price (zero for pilot), terms, exit                                |
| 7   | Why us          | Three differentiators tied to their pain                           |
| 8   | Next step       | One concrete CTA with date                                         |

Keep it under 4 pages PDF. Long proposals lose to short ones.

Date: {{date}}

{{2-4 sentences in their words about the pain: paperwork hours, parent communication, data fragmentation, etc.}}

## Template 4 — District / Government Proposal (stub)

For multi-school deals with a ministry or district office, the template above is wrong. Government deals require:

| Section                 | Source                                                                                |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Compliance posture      | FERPA, GDPR, regional data residency                                                  |
| Procurement vehicle     | RFP response, sole-source justification, framework agreement                          |
| Volume pricing          | ENTERPRISE tier from [Business Model](/docs/business-model) — $1.00 / student / month |
| Implementation services | From [Business Model](/docs/business-model) — $5K–$100K SoW range                     |
| SLA                     | 99.9% uptime, defined penalties                                                       |
| Multi-year terms        | Year 1 + 2 optional + 3 optional structure                                            |

Until our first district deal closes, this template is a checklist — draft per opportunity with Ali, Sedon, and Abdout reviewing.

## Customization checklist

Before sending any proposal, confirm every variable is filled:

- [ ] `{{school_name}}` — formal name in school's preferred language
- [ ] `{{contact_name}}` and `{{role}}`
- [ ] `{{owner}}` and `{{owner_email}}` — usually Ali for pilots, Abdout for paid
- [ ] `{{date}}` — today's date, plus 14 days for `{{proposed_date}}`
- [ ] `{{slug}}` — subdomain (lowercase, hyphenated)
- [ ] `{{student_count}}` — confirmed with school, not guessed
- [ ] Curriculum seed — Sudan / Saudi / generic — matches school's curriculum
- [ ] Pain section uses 2–4 sentences from the school's own discovery call notes
- [ ] PDF rendered, branded, under 4 pages
- [ ] Sent as PDF attachment + paragraph summary in email body

## Sending playbook

| Day    | Action                                                                        |
| ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Day 0  | Send proposal email (PDF + 3-sentence summary in body) to primary + cc        |
| Day 3  | Soft check-in — "any questions on the proposal?" (one line, no PDF re-attach) |
| Day 7  | Decision-maker follow-up — propose a 15-min call to resolve any blocker       |
| Day 14 | Last touch — confirm if interest is still active or close the loop            |
| Day 21 | If silent — move to `lost` in [Leads](/docs/leads), log reason                |

Three contacts max. After Day 21, stop. Quality outreach is finite; channel future effort to the next prospect.
