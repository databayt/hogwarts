A lead is a named school with a named contact in a target MENA country. Anything less is a prospect or a hope. This page is the operational layer beneath [Pilot](/docs/pilot) and [Sales](/docs/sales) — how a school enters the funnel, moves through stages, and either converts or exits. The single source of truth for the live pipeline is [`kun/.claude/memory/customers.json`](https://github.com/databayt/kun/blob/main/.claude/memory/customers.json).

## Lead vs prospect vs cold

| Status   | Definition                                                                | Example                                   |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Cold     | School name known, no contact named, no message sent                      | "There's a private school in Madani"      |
| Prospect | School name + contact named, target country, no conversation yet          | "Mr. Ahmed at School X, sent LinkedIn DM" |
| Lead     | First message sent and school has responded (positively or with question) | "Ahmed Baha replied — wants a demo"       |
| Pilot    | Pilot agreement signed                                                    | King Fahad Schools today                  |
| Paid     | Paid contract active                                                      | none today                                |

Counting prospects as leads inflates the funnel and hides the real bottleneck. Be honest.

## Lifecycle

```
cold → prospect → warm (reply) → discovery → demo → trial → pilot → paid
                                                          ↘
                                                            extension (12 mo)
```

| Stage     | Defined by                                           | Exit gate                                  |
| --------- | ---------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| cold      | Identified school, no contact attempt yet            | Find and verify primary contact            |
| prospect  | Contact named, country in MENA-10                    | Send first message                         |
| warm      | School has replied                                   | School agrees to discovery call            |
| discovery | 30-min call held; pain captured in 3–5 sentences     | School agrees to a demo                    |
| demo      | 30-min live product demo, role-specific views shown  | School agrees to 14-day trial              |
| trial     | School poking around on demo subdomain               | School asks "what's next?" — propose       |
| pilot     | Free Pilot Proposal signed                           | Subdomain provisioned + Day 0 deliverables |
| paid      | Paid Conversion Proposal signed                      | First invoice paid                         |
| lost      | School declined or went silent ≥21 days at any stage | Log reason in `lost_reason` field          |

Movement is one-way except `pilot → extension` and `lost → re-engaged` (a school can come back, capture the reason).

## Discovery call script

Thirty minutes. Sixty percent listening. Goals: capture pain in their words, identify the decision maker, qualify out if not a fit.

| #   | Question                                                                                     | What you're listening for                       |
| --- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Tell me how the school year started this month — what's working, what's hard?                | Open the conversation, hear pain in their words |
| 2   | How are you handling enrollment / attendance / parent communication today?                   | Current toolset (Excel, WhatsApp, paper)        |
| 3   | What's the biggest time-sink for you or your admin team each week?                           | Paperwork hours number — quantify the pain      |
| 4   | Who else is part of the decision when you change school software?                            | Identify the real decision maker                |
| 5   | If we could give the platform free for 6 months, what would we need to do to make this work? | Hidden objections, fit signals                  |
| 6   | What does success look like for you in 6 months?                                             | Their definition of value — write it down       |
| 7   | What's your honest concern about doing this?                                                 | Surface objections early                        |

Close: "I'd like to show you the platform — 30 minutes next week. Tuesday or Thursday?" Get the demo on the calendar before the call ends.

Anti-pattern: pitching during discovery. If you find yourself talking more than they are at minute 15, you have stopped doing discovery.

## Follow-up cadence

| Channel                 | Day 1                            | Day 3                     | Day 7                     | Day 14                    | Day 21                     |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| LinkedIn DM first touch | Opener — 3 sentences, 1 question | (no message)              | Soft check-in — 1 line    | Last touch — propose call | Close loop, move to `lost` |
| WhatsApp first touch    | Voice note or 2-line text        | (no message)              | Soft check-in             | Propose call              | Close loop                 |
| Email first touch       | 4-paragraph email + 1 CTA        | (no message)              | Reply-bump on same thread | New thread with new angle | Close loop                 |
| After discovery call    | Thank-you + 2 things you heard   | Demo invitation           | (no message)              | Confirm demo or close     | —                          |
| After demo              | Thank-you + trial subdomain link | (no message)              | Trial check-in            | Propose pilot or close    | —                          |
| After proposal sent     | Proposal email + summary         | "Any questions?" — 1 line | Decision-maker follow-up  | Last touch                | Close loop                 |

Three to four touches max in any 21-day window. Quality outreach is finite. After Day 21 silence, stop — channel future effort to the next prospect. Re-engagement is allowed after 90 days with a new angle (a shipped feature, a case study, a referral).

## Anti-patterns

| Don't                                                 | Do instead                                                |
| ----------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Multi-touch in one day (DM + email + WhatsApp in 24h) | One channel per touch; rotate channels across the cadence |
| Spam with same template across 10 schools             | One handwritten paragraph per school                      |
| Lead with the pitch                                   | Lead with one question about their pain                   |
| Promise features we haven't shipped                   | Show what's live; mark roadmap items as roadmap           |
| Mark a prospect as "lead" because they liked a post   | Wait for a real reply or a meeting                        |
| Re-engage `lost` schools inside 90 days               | Wait 90 days minimum, come back with a new angle          |
| Hide a lost deal from `customers.json`                | Log it. Reasons compound into the strategy.               |
