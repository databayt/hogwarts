Expenses lets staff submit costs, routes them through approval, deducts approved amounts from [budget](/docs/finance-budget) allocations, and processes reimbursement. Categories are managed alongside.

## Ledger / accounting

**Does not post to the ledger.** `postExpensePayment` exists in the engine but has zero callers, so approving/paying an expense does not create journal entries (the README's `lib/accounting/` claim is aspirational). Approval does, however, decrement the linked budget allocation.
