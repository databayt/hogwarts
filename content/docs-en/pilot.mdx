Hogwarts is opening **10 free pilot slots** for K-12 schools across MENA. Pilots run 6 months on the live platform with full support, in exchange for weekly feedback and the right to publish a case study. The first slot is held by [King Fahad Schools](https://kingfahad.databayt.org). For lead tracking see [Leads](/docs/leads); for proposal templates see [Proposal](/docs/proposal); for the wider commercial process see [Sales](/docs/sales).

## Why a free pilot cohort

We are [default dead](/docs/business-model) with ten months of runway and one product that is 95% complete. The fastest path to a real revenue engine is not one perfect customer — it is ten engaged schools generating bug reports, feature requests, testimonials, and case studies in parallel. More pilots → faster product polish → trust → paid expansion. This is [doing things that don't scale](https://paulgraham.com/ds.html) at startup time, exactly as it should be.

This frame also aligns with our [Shared Economy](/docs/shared-economy) posture — pilot schools are early users who shape the product, not customers we ship a finished product to.

## The offer

| What schools get                            | What schools commit to                             |
| ------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| 6 months free on the live platform          | Weekly 30-min feedback call (Arabic or English)    |
| Dedicated subdomain (`school.databayt.org`) | ≥3 issue reports / month on GitHub or via email    |
| Seeded curriculum (Sudan / Saudi / generic) | Permission to feature logo as "pilot partner"      |
| Onboarding support (Day 1, 14, 30)          | Participation in a published case study at month 6 |
| Priority access to founding team            | Honest signal — if it's not working, tell us       |
| Arabic-first UI, full RTL                   | No public criticism without prior conversation     |

**Pilot to paid path**: month 6 conversion proposal at 30% off PRO tier ($1.05/student/month, locked for 12 months) + co-design partnership status.

**Exit terms**: schools can leave any time. We export all data in standard CSV/JSON. No lock-in.

## Recruitment funnel

| Stage           | Definition                                                          | Conversion target |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| 1. Identified   | Named school, named contact, country in MENA-10 list                | 100 / month       |
| 2. Approached   | First message sent (LinkedIn, WhatsApp, email)                      | 30%               |
| 3. Discovery    | 30-min call held; pain confirmed; budget irrelevant (pilot is free) | 25%               |
| 4. Trial        | 14-day evaluation on demo school subdomain                          | 60%               |
| 5. Pilot signed | Agreement signed; school's own subdomain provisioned                | 70%               |
| 6. Active       | School is using the platform with ≥10 students entered              | 90%               |

End-to-end conversion target: identified → active ≈ **2.4%**. To land 10 pilots we need to identify ~420 schools.

## Pilot-school responsibilities

- **Weekly feedback call** — 30 minutes, scheduled by school. Can be skipped twice without consequence.
- **GitHub issues** — at least 3 / month, any kind (bug, feature request, polish, doc gap). Posted to [databayt/hogwarts](https://github.com/databayt/hogwarts/issues) or emailed to support.
- **Testimonial** — one quote suitable for [Case Studies](/docs/case-study) at month 6. School controls wording.
- **Logo permission** — school logo on hogwarts.databayt.org marketing pages and in proposals to future prospects.
- **No public criticism without prior conversation** — if something is broken, tell us first. Public criticism after we have failed to respond in 7 days is fair.

## Risks and mitigations

| Risk                                       | Mitigation                                                                |
| ------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Pilots churn before month 6                | Weekly feedback call is the anchor — disengagement is visible fast        |
| Feedback overload — too many issues to fix | Captain reviews issue volume weekly; reject features that don't help MENA |
| No pilot converts to paid                  | Conversion conversation starts month 3, not month 6 — early warning       |
| Free pilots eat support time               | Sedon's batched-weekly support model; Day 0–30 playbook is repeatable     |
| Single Sudan-only cohort = concentration   | MENA-10 spans 6 countries — geographic diversification is built in        |

## Conversion to paid

At month 6 each pilot receives a [Conversion Proposal](/docs/proposal). Standard terms:

| Item          | Pilot offer          | Paid year 1 (post-pilot)                      |
| ------------- | -------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Price         | $0                   | $1.05/student/month (30% off PRO, 12-mo lock) |
| Tier          | PRO features         | PRO + co-design partner status                |
| Support       | Founding team direct | Founding team direct                          |
| Roadmap input | Weekly call          | Quarterly roadmap session                     |
| Logo on site  | Pilot partner        | Founding partner                              |

If a school declines conversion, we offer a 12-month extension at $0 with reduced support (monthly call instead of weekly) for 5 of the 10 slots — to preserve the case study while we wait for budget.
