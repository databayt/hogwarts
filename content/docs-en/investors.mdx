## Executive summary

- **Stage**: pre-seed bridge.
- **Target raise**: $250K-$500K across instruments (grant + SAFE-cap + accelerator stipend blended).
- **Instrument preference**: SAFE (cap, no discount preferred; up to 20% discount acceptable).
- **Valuation cap stance**: $4M-$6M post-money (anchor — adjust per round dynamics).
- **MFN**: yes.
- **Pro-rata**: yes (standard).
- **Info rights**: monthly + on-demand.
- **Board seat at this stage**: no.

## The opportunity

**Why now**: Sudan reopened schools in February 2026 after a three-year hiatus. Saudi Vision 2030 + NTDP is deploying $430M+ across new initiatives in 2026 with EdTech named as priority. MENA EdTech is sized at $14.4B → $33B by 2030. The market is being built right now, and the incumbent vendors are not Arabic-first.

**Why this**: Hogwarts is a complete cloud-based school operating system — one platform for enrollment, attendance, exams, timetables, grading, finance, parent communication. Arabic-first, RTL-native, multi-tenant. Open source under SSPL-1.0. We have a live paying-pilot at King Fahad Schools, a public demo, 4,912 source files, and AI integrations wired (Anthropic, OpenAI, LangChain).

**Why us**: The team is Sudanese-founded, distributed across Sudan / KSA / Egypt / Europe. We've shipped 14+ epics across the MVP scope (admission, attendance, exams, timetable, grades, finance, parent portal, transportation, stream/video, notifications, payments). We work bilingual native. We have the cultural context and the technical depth — neither side without the other works in this market.

## Toxic terms — we will NOT accept

- Full ratchet anti-dilution
- Participating preference > 1× (or "double-dip")
- Board control at seed (any majority-vote arrangement that requires investor sign-off on operational decisions)
- Founder vesting acceleration only on "good leaver" — single-trigger only
- Drag-along below 60% threshold
- Restrictive covenants on the open-source license (we will not narrow SSPL-1.0)
- "No-shop" clauses longer than 30 days
- Personal guarantees from founders

## Process

1. **Intro** — warm preferred; cold tolerated with high-signal email (T1 in [Outreach](/docs/outreach)).
2. **30-min discovery call** — product walkthrough on demo + traction page + 2 questions for the investor (their thesis, their portfolio overlap).
3. **Data room access** — Tier 1 only on first ask; Tier 2 (financials, cap table) under mutual NDA. See [Data Room](/docs/data-room).
4. **Partner meeting** — second call, full deck, partners present.
5. **Term sheet** — we counter with the term-sheet stance above.
6. **Diligence** — 2-4 weeks; technical DD via GitHub + architecture review; customer DD via King Fahad reference call.
7. **Close** — SAFE signed, wire received, intro to ecosystem.

Target timeline from first intro to close: **6-10 weeks**.

## Things we will say no to (in the room)

- **Renegotiation after term sheet** without a material new finding. We honor signed paper.
- **Exclusive negotiating periods longer than 30 days**.
- **Mandatory pivots** as a condition of investment.
- **"We invest after you have $X MRR"** when $X moves the goalposts during the conversation. We accept "not now" once; we don't accept moving milestones.

## Things we are honest about (in the room)

- $0 MRR today.
- 1 pilot is not a market.
- Conversion is the gate.
- We will not promise unit economics we haven't yet proven.
- We are not the world's best EdTech founder team. We are the right team for **Arabic-first, open-source, multi-tenant K-12 SaaS in MENA**. Different question.
