## Reference

The condensed essentials. Full detail lives in the linked docs.

**Stage** — Pre-seed bridge. Seed milestone is 10 paying schools + $50K MRR. Until that lands we raise non-dilutive + small SAFE only.

**Target** — $250K–$500K total across instruments over the next 12 months. Goal is to maximize runway-per-dilution — every dollar of grant + credit + accelerator stipend is a dollar we don't sell equity for.

**Use of funds**

| Bucket                              | %   | 12-month deliverable                                                       |
| ----------------------------------- | --- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Engineering (1 senior hire)         | 35% | Payment-umbrella P0 / P1 + offline engine + AI tutor MVP                   |
| MENA sales (1 lead + 1 SDR)         | 30% | MENA-10 conversion → 10 paying schools → MENA-25 cohort                    |
| Pilot subsidies (free for 6 months) | 15% | 10 free pilots fully serviced incl. onboarding visits                      |
| Compliance + entity setup           | 10% | KSA legal entity, Sudan registration, third-party security audit, SSO/SAML |

**What we will NOT do**

- **Premature equity dilution.** A priced seed at < $50K MRR signals weakness; we wait.
- **Pivot-demanding programs.** If a program requires us to abandon Arabic-first or open-source positioning, we walk.
- **Debt.** Not appropriate at our cash position; revisit only when we have collateral revenue.
- **Revenue-share deals that lock the open-source license.** The SSPL-1.0 commitment is structural.
- **Toxic terms in any equity instrument** — full ratchet anti-dilution, > 1× participating prefs, board control at seed.
- **Pay-to-pitch.** Any "accelerator" or competition that asks for an application fee > $50 is a filter.

**Why now** — Live paying-pilot ([King Fahad Schools](https://kingfahad.databayt.org)), public demo ([demo.databayt.org](https://demo.databayt.org)), 4,912 TS/TSX files + 52 tests, AR/EN i18n parity (2,247 / 2,243 strings), MVP across 14+ epics, Sudan schools reopened Feb 2026 after 3-year hiatus, MENA EdTech funding +169% YoY in Q1 2025. Full table in [Traction](/docs/traction).
