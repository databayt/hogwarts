This is the **strategy hub** for every form of external support we seek — capital, accelerator placement, grants, sponsor credits, partnerships. Every doc in this section is downstream of decisions made here.

_Last verified: 2026-05-18._

## Executive summary

We are post-MVP with one live pilot ([King Fahad Schools](https://kingfahad.databayt.org)). We are not pre-product, and not a deck. We've earned eligibility for outside support and we go after it deliberately, in this order:

1. **Non-dilutive first** — grants (NTDP, Mastercard Foundation EdTech, UNESCO Sudan TEP), vendor credits (Microsoft, Google, AWS, Anthropic, OpenAI), incubator stipends (Orange Corners, MSAR).
2. **Accelerators second** — KSA-anchored (Sanabil 500, Misk500, Flat6Labs Riyadh, Hub71), global EdTech (Mastercard EdTech Fellowship, OpenAI Grove, YC).
3. **Equity last** — SAFE-cap from MENA EdTech-aware VCs (Owl Ventures, STV, Wamda, Raed, Wa'ed) only after non-dilutive routes are exhausted or insufficient for the milestone.

The point of this sequence: **maximize the runway-per-dilution ratio**. Every dollar of grant + credit + accelerator stipend is a dollar we don't sell equity for.

## What we're raising

**Stage**: Pre-seed bridge. We are not raising a priced seed yet. The seed milestone is **10 paying schools + $50K+ MRR** — until that is in hand, we raise non-dilutive + small SAFE.

**Target**: $250K-$500K total across instruments over the next 12 months.

**Use of funds**:

| Bucket                              | %   | 12-month deliverable                                                       |
| ----------------------------------- | --- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Engineering (1 senior hire)         | 35% | Payment-umbrella P0/P1 + offline engine + AI tutor MVP                     |
| MENA sales (1 lead + 1 SDR)         | 30% | MENA-10 conversion → 10 paying schools → MENA-25 cohort                    |
| Pilot subsidies (free for 6 months) | 15% | 10 free pilots fully serviced incl. onboarding visits                      |
| Compliance + entity setup           | 10% | KSA legal entity, Sudan registration, third-party security audit, SSO/SAML |
| Runway buffer                       | 10% | 2-month operational reserve                                                |

## What we will NOT do

- **Premature equity dilution.** A priced seed at < $50K MRR signals weakness; we wait.
- **Pivot-demanding incubators.** If a program requires us to abandon Arabic-first or open-source positioning, we walk.
- **Debt.** Not appropriate at our cash position; revisit only when we have collateral revenue.
- **Revenue-share deals that lock the open-source license.** The SSPL-1.0 commitment is structural.
- **Toxic terms in any equity instrument** — full ratchet anti-dilution, > 1× participating prefs, board control at seed. List lives in [Investors](/docs/investors).
- **Pay-to-pitch.** Any "accelerator" that asks for an application fee > $0 is a filter.

## Decision cadence

- **Weekly captain review** — pipeline state across audiences; new applications; replies received; follow-ups due.
- **Monthly checkpoint** — match against [GTM](/docs/gtm) phase gates. If MRR or pilot count missed, do not advance to the next instrument ladder rung.
- **Quarterly fundraising review** — re-baseline use-of-funds, milestones, runway, and the entire instrument ladder. Refresh [Deck](/docs/deck) + [Data Room](/docs/data-room).

## Phase gate against GTM

[GTM](/docs/gtm) defines three phases (Sudan beachhead → MENA-10 conversion → MENA-25 expansion). Each phase has its own fundraising posture:

| GTM phase                    | Fundraising posture                                         |
| ---------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Phase 1 — Sudan beachhead    | Grants + credits + incubator stipends only                  |
| Phase 2 — MENA-10 conversion | + Accelerator (zero-equity), SAFE (cap) from MENA-aware VCs |
| Phase 3 — MENA-25 expansion  | + Priced seed (after 10 paying schools, $50K+ MRR)          |

Today we are in **Phase 2**. We do not run Phase 3 fundraising on Phase 1 evidence.
