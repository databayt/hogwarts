A ready-to-ship content kit for the **Digital Admission Module**, built for the Monday "product feature in Arabic" slot in the [Marketing](/docs/marketing) calendar. The angle is **massive time reduction**: a school's 20–30 minutes of manual data entry per student collapses into a one-tap review. Copy is provided in English and Arabic, with a 30-second screen-capture script and posting notes.

## The angle

|             |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| ----------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Pain**    | Parents fill thick paper folders by hand. The secretary retypes every line into a spreadsheet — 20–30 minutes per student, hundreds of times each September — plus the typos and the parent calls to fix them.                                                        |
| **Promise** | The parent fills one digital form from their phone. The moment they submit, the application lands in the registry — fully structured, zero retyping. The admin reviews and approves with one tap, and the student profile + tuition plan are generated automatically. |
| **Metric**  | **30 minutes of manual entry → a 2-minute review.**                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |

## Accuracy & setup notes

Read before publishing or recording — these keep the claims defensible.

- **The "2 minutes" is the school's side.** The 30 minutes was the secretary's per-student typing; the product removes that data entry entirely, leaving roughly a 2-minute review-and-approve. The parent still fills the form once on their phone (realistically ~5 minutes). Keep the metric on the school's time — don't claim the parent finishes in 2 minutes.
- **"Tuition generated automatically" needs fee structures in place.** The auto-allocation matches the student's grade/year against existing fee structures. Configure them on the demo school **before** recording, or the tuition panel renders empty. See [Admission](/docs/admission) and [Fees](/docs/fees).
- **"Without a single manual entry" is true for school staff.** They never retype student data — the parent enters it once; the registry populates on submit; enrollment is a one-tap confirm.
