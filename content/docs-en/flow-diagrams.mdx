## 4. Login

Two distinct contexts:

- **SaaS marketing login** (`localhost:3000` / `ed.databayt.org`) ‚Äî only `DEVELOPER` redirects to the SaaS dashboard. Everyone else stays on the SaaS marketing page; school members should login from their own subdomain. We do NOT auto-redirect school members to their school dashboard.
- **School marketing login** (`demo.localhost:3000` / `demo.databayt.org`) ‚Äî users stay on the school marketing page after login. Dashboard access is via the "Platform" header link, which checks membership.

If a school member accidentally logs in on the SaaS main domain and tries `/dashboard`, they hit `/access-denied` ‚Äî the SaaS dashboard is `DEVELOPER`-only by design.

<LoginFlow />

Tests: `login.spec.ts` (AUTH-001 to AUTH-022), `saas-login-flow.spec.ts`.

## 5. Logout

Context-aware logout that preserves locale and stays on the current domain. `LogoutButton` always navigates to `/{locale}` (e.g. `/en`); the proxy rewrites this to `/en/s/{subdomain}/` on a school subdomain. This avoids double-rewrite bugs.

<LogoutFlow />

Rules:

- Locale never changes during logout.
- `/onboarding` is never a logout redirect target.
- `signOut({ redirect: false })` ‚Äî the auth.ts redirect callback is NOT invoked during logout.

Tests: `logout.spec.ts` (AUTH-038 to AUTH-045).

## 6. DEVELOPER dashboard

Two access paths to `ed.databayt.org/en/dashboard`. Two security layers:

1. **Proxy middleware** ‚Äî extracts role from JWT, blocks non-`DEVELOPER` at the edge.
2. **Layout server component** ‚Äî full `auth()` check, redirects non-`DEVELOPER` to `/access-denied`.

<DeveloperDashboardFlow />

Tests: `saas-dashboard.spec.ts` (EP-017 to EP-022), `saas-login-flow.spec.ts`.
