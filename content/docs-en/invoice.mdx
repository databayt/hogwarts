The invoice module is the school's central billing surface. Invoices are created manually through a wizard or generated automatically by every billable event (enrollment fees, transport route fees, course/video purchases), rendered as a print-first minimalist document, and delivered by public share link, PDF, or email. Mutations gate through the finance guard (`requireFinanceActor`); reads are owner-scoped so students and guardians always reach their own invoices.

## How invoices are generated

| Source                     | Trigger                                                | Result                                                            |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Manual                     | "Create Invoice" → wizard                              | Draft in the school currency with a prefilled sequential number   |
| Enrollment / student added | `ensureStudentFeeAssignments` → `fee-invoice-sync`     | `ENR-` invoices per fee assignment, itemized per fee component    |
| Transport route fees       | "Provision to billing" on the transportation fees page | Per route + month structure → assignments → invoices (idempotent) |
| Course / video purchase    | Stripe webhook on successful checkout                  | `PUR-` invoice already marked PAID (retry-idempotent)             |

Registration fees collected at offer acceptance are allocated onto their auto-generated invoice inside the enrollment transaction, so the invoice shows PAID the moment enrollment completes.

### Itemization

Single-installment invoices carry one line per non-zero fee component (tuition, admission, registration, exam, library, laboratory, sports, transport, hostel, plus custom `otherFees`), labeled in the school's language. When a scholarship bills below list price the gap lands in the invoice discount field; billing above adds an adjustment line. Multi-installment invoices keep one "Installment N of M" line each.

## Print, share, PDF

- **Print** — the document view and the public share page both print as a clean A4 sheet via the browser (app chrome is stripped by global print styles).
- **Share** — the Share dialog mints a public link (`/invoice/{token}`); anyone with the link can view and print, no account needed. Revoking disables access but keeps the token, so re-enabling restores the exact same URL. Send via WhatsApp or email straight from the dialog.
- **PDF** — Download PDF renders the same minimalist design via `@react-pdf` (IBM Plex Mono for English, Rubik for Arabic).
- **Email** — the Resend email is bilingual and links the public page when sharing is enabled, otherwise the authed detail view.

## Accounting

`markInvoicePaid` posts DR Cash / CR Accounts Receivable to the double-entry ledger (race-safe, keyed on the invoice id). Purchase invoices are created without a journal entry for now — a point-of-sale posting rule (DR Cash / CR Revenue) is tracked as a follow-up.
