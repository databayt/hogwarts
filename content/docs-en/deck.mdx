## Slide 2 — Problem

Seventeen million children are out of school in Sudan. Schools that have reopened — and the broader MENA system serving 80M+ K-12 students — run on paper registers, Excel sheets, and WhatsApp groups.

| Pain                                    | Industry baseline                 |
| --------------------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Admin time lost to paperwork            | 40+ hours/month per administrator |
| Cost of fragmented tooling              | $15K–$50K/year per school         |
| Data error rate from manual entry       | ~23%                              |
| Parents dissatisfied with communication | ~65%                              |

The problem is documented. The buyers are reachable. The disruption window is open.

## Slide 3 — Solution

A complete cloud-based school operating system. One platform for enrollment, attendance, exams, timetables, grading, finance, parent communication. Arabic-first. Multi-tenant. Open source.

| Capability             | What schools get                                          |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Multi-tenant SaaS      | A new school onboards in minutes; isolation by `schoolId` |
| Arabic-first / RTL     | Built RTL-native, not bolted on                           |
| AI-assisted operations | Document review, exam wizard, bank-receipt extraction     |
| Open source (SSPL-1.0) | Self-host free; managed SaaS for those who prefer not to  |

## Slide 4 — Why now

| Tailwind                                                                    | Why it matters                                                  |
| --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Sudan reopened schools in Feb 2026 after a 3-year hiatus                    | Schools rebuild from zero infrastructure — no incumbent         |
| Saudi Vision 2030 + NTDP ($430M+ deployed in 2026)                          | Government is buying tech infrastructure today                  |

## Slide 8 — Business model

Tiered SaaS. Free → Pro → Enterprise.

| Tier       | Price                   | Target                             |
| ---------- | ----------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Free       | $0 (up to 100 students) | Smallest schools, NGOs, pilots     |
| Pro        | $1.50/student/month     | Private K-12 (300-2,000 students)  |
| Enterprise | $1.00/student/month     | School groups, ministries (2,000+) |
| Services   | Project-based           | Migration, custom integration      |

5-year revenue projection: Y1 $240K → Y3 $6M → Y5 $36M. See [Business model](/docs/business-model) for the full unit economics and assumptions.

## Slide 11 — Ask

| Bucket                              | %   | 12-month deliverable                                           |
| ----------------------------------- | --- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Engineering (1 senior hire)         | 35% | Payment-umbrella P0/P1 + offline engine + AI tutor MVP         |
