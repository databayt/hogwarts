## Executive summary

Hogwarts is a SaaS school management platform — 95% complete, live at [ed.databayt.org](https://ed.databayt.org). It automates enrollment, attendance, exams, timetables, grading, finance, and parent communication for K-12 schools. Arabic-first, RTL-native, multi-tenant. Open source.

Sudan first; MENA expansion second. Schools reopening after the 2023–2025 conflict need digital infrastructure from day one — there is no incumbent to displace.

## Sudan education context

The civil war (April 2023 – present) devastated Sudan's education system. Khartoum resumed its first school year in February 2026 after a three-year hiatus. Schools are reopening with zero digital infrastructure.

| Metric                                 | Value |
| -------------------------------------- | ----- |
| Children affected                      | 17M   |
| Days of learning lost                  | 500+  |
| Schools reopened                       | ~35%  |
| UNESCO Education Plan 2025–2027 budget | $580M |

This is the context, not a constraint. The market is being built right now.

## The opportunity

- **Problem.** Schools manage operations with paper registers, Excel sheets, WhatsApp groups. Administrators lose 40+ hours / month to manual entry. Records are fragmented; parents are disconnected.
- **Solution.** A complete cloud-based school OS — one platform for enrollment, attendance, exams, timetables, grading, finance, parent communication. Arabic-first, mobile-responsive, priced for emerging markets.
- **Traction.** 200K+ lines of code, 514 passing tests, multi-tenant architecture, full RTL, open source on GitHub.
- **Business model.** Tiered SaaS — Free up to 100 students, Pro $1.50/student/month, Enterprise $1.00/student/month. See [Business Model](/docs/business-model).
- **The ask.** Incubation support to land first 5 pilot schools, MENA go-to-market mentorship, seed funding to bridge to first revenue.
