Budget defines spending envelopes, allocates them across categories, and tracks spent-vs-allocated variance. [Expenses](/docs/finance-expenses) draw down allocations on approval; the resulting variance feeds [reports](/docs/finance-reports).

## Ledger / accounting

Not a ledger money-mover. Budget tracks planned vs actual spend; the "actual" side is decremented by expense approvals, not by journal postings.
