_Last verified: 2026-05-18._

## Executive summary

Vendor credits are the easiest, fastest dollar in fundraising — no equity, no dilution, no reporting overhead. The total face-value of the stack below is approximately **$750K** with **1-2 days of cumulative admin work**. Apply to all that fit. Some are unlocked by being in an accelerator/VC partner network; those wait until [Accelerators](/docs/accelerators) lands an "Accepted" status.

## Apply sequence

Order matters. Some programs unlock dozens of others via partner chains.

1. **Stripe Atlas** ($500 incorporation) → unlocks 50+ perks chain (AWS $5K, Notion 6mo, MongoDB $5K, OpenAI $2.5K, HubSpot, Carta, Deel).
2. **Microsoft for Startups Founders Hub** — direct apply, no VC required.
3. **Google for Startups Cloud Program** — direct apply (MENA tier).
4. **AWS Activate Founders** — direct $1K → upgrade to $100K Portfolio after first accelerator/VC.
5. **Vercel for Startups, Linear, Notion, Supabase, Figma** — direct apply individually.
6. **GitHub for Startups, Anthropic Startup, OpenAI Grove, Datadog, Sentry** — partner-gated; queued behind Phase 2/3 accelerator/VC progress.

## Application workflow

For each credit/sponsor program:

1. **Read program eligibility** — most require: an entity (LLC, FZE, or equivalent), > 0 LOC of code, a working product URL.
2. **Pick the right tier** — many programs have multiple tiers; the highest tier needs accelerator/VC affiliation.
3. **Fill the form** using the variable boilerplate in T5 ([Outreach](/docs/outreach)).
4. **Log status** in the table above immediately; some credits expire if not deployed within N months.
5. **Renew annually** — most programs renew on anniversary; set calendar reminders.

## What we will NOT accept

- Credit programs that require closing-source any portion of our code.
- Sponsors that demand exclusive payment processing (we keep Stripe + Plaid + Tap).
- CSR partnerships that require us to brand the platform under their name (we keep Hogwarts branding).
- Programs that lock us into a 3+ year platform commitment (vendor lock-in risk).
