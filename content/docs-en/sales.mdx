| Stage        | Goal              | Activity                                                                    |

| 1. Discovery | Qualify           | Research school size, decision makers, current systems, budget              |

## Objection handling

### "We already have a system"

Many schools we work with had existing tools too. The difference: Hogwarts is built for your region from day one with Arabic-first design and multi-tenant isolation. We can show you exactly where your current setup leaves data exposed across tenants.

### "It's too expensive"

A typical school saves 40 admin hours/month — nearly a full-time equivalent. At $1.50/student/month, the platform pays for itself within 2–3 months through operational savings alone.

### "We need to involve the ministry / board"

This is an important decision. We run 3-month pilots specifically designed for stakeholder review — measure metrics, document results, present to the board. Confidence first, commitment second.

### "What about data security?"

Each school is fully isolated via `schoolId` scoping on every database query. We're FERPA + GDPR compliant, AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, with complete audit logs. Annual penetration testing, bug bounty.
