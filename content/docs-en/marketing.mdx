Marketing for Hogwarts is content-first, Arabic-first, and pilot-driven. We do not buy ads in 2026; we build evidence. Every piece of content either teaches a school admin something useful, demonstrates the product, or amplifies a pilot case study. For execution-level workflows the canonical agent is [`kun/.claude/agents/growth.md`](https://github.com/databayt/kun/blob/main/.claude/agents/growth.md). For where marketing meets sales see [GTM](/docs/gtm); for pilot recruitment hooks see [Pilot](/docs/pilot).

## Positioning

> Arabic-first, open-source school OS for MENA — built for schools coming online, not schools upgrading legacy systems.

| Pillar                | What it means                                                           | Evidence                                                   |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Arabic-first          | RTL-native, every screen, every role, every email                       | [Internationalization](/docs/internationalization) docs    |
| Open-source           | SSPL, public roadmap, public failures, contributors as owners           | [Shared economy](/docs/shared-economy)                     |
| MENA-built            | Sudan-pilot first, founding team rooted in the region                   | [King Fahad Schools pilot](https://kingfahad.databayt.org) |
| Schools coming online | Built for post-conflict, emerging-market, Arabic-speaking K-12 contexts | [Incubators](/docs/incubators) Sudan context               |

We are not the cheapest, not the biggest, not the most polished. We are the most aligned with how MENA private schools actually operate.

## Audience segments

| Segment                  | Who they are                           | Channel                          | What they care about                       |
| ------------------------ | -------------------------------------- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Private K-12 owners      | 1–3 schools, $50K–$500K annual revenue | LinkedIn (Arabic), WhatsApp      | Hours saved, parent satisfaction, cost     |
| School administrators    | Day-to-day operators                   | LinkedIn (Arabic), peer referral | Paperwork, attendance, parent calls        |
| Government procurement   | Ministry / district decision makers    | RFP + introductions              | Compliance, multi-school, custom contracts |
| Edtech partners          | Content providers, training centres    | Direct outreach, conferences     | Integration, revenue share                 |
| Open-source contributors | MENA developers, students, hobbyists   | GitHub, Twitter, Discord         | Stack, problems to solve, recognition      |
| Parent associations      | Influential community voices           | Pilot school relationships       | Communication quality, transparency        |

The first three are buying audiences. The last three are amplifier audiences — they create the trust signal that makes the first three respond.

## Content calendar

Weekly rhythm, owned by Samia under the [`growth`](https://github.com/databayt/kun/blob/main/.claude/agents/growth.md) agent. Each day has a default content type so production stays predictable.

| Day       | Content type                            | Audience               | Channel            | Example                              |
| --------- | --------------------------------------- | ---------------------- | ------------------ | ------------------------------------ |
| Monday    | Product feature in Arabic               | Admins, owners         | LinkedIn AR, X     | "كيف تخفض ساعات الإدارة بـ 80٪"      |
| Tuesday   | Technical post                          | Contributors, partners | LinkedIn EN, X, GH | "Multi-tenant routing in Next.js 16" |
| Wednesday | Community / shared-economy              | All amplifiers         | LinkedIn EN, X     | "Our 9th contributor shipped …"      |
| Thursday  | Pilot case study or admin story         | Owners, admins         | LinkedIn AR + EN   | "Inside King Fahad's first month"    |
| Friday    | Developer relations / behind-the-scenes | Contributors, students | X, GitHub, Discord | "Friday demo — exam wizard"          |

Each piece is tagged with a primary SEO target (see below) and reused: LinkedIn post → blog → X thread → docs example. One artifact, four surfaces.

## SEO targets

Keyword targeting is half Arabic, half English, weighted toward Arabic for MENA share. Top-of-funnel keywords are aspirational (`نظام إدارة مدرسة عربي`); bottom-of-funnel keywords are commercial (`Hogwarts school management pricing`).

| Keyword (Arabic)             | Volume (est.) | Intent         | Status    |
| ---------------------------- | ------------- | -------------- | --------- |
| نظام إدارة مدرسة             | High          | Awareness      | Targeting |
| برنامج إدارة مدارس عربي      | Medium        | Comparison     | Targeting |
| نظام مدرسي مفتوح المصدر      | Low           | Differentiator | Owned     |
| إدارة الحضور والغياب للمدارس | Medium        | Feature        | Targeting |
| رسوم مدارس برنامج            | Low           | Commercial     | Targeting |

| Keyword (English)               | Volume (est.) | Intent         | Status    |
| ------------------------------- | ------------- | -------------- | --------- |
| Arabic school management system | Medium        | Awareness      | Targeting |
| Open source school SIS          | Low           | Differentiator | Owned     |
| MENA school management software | Low           | Comparison     | Targeting |
| Multi-tenant LMS Next.js        | Niche         | Contributor    | Owned     |
| Hogwarts school management      | Low           | Branded        | Owned     |

The docs site (you are reading it) is the SEO host. Every doc page is a permanent landing surface; each one targets one keyword cluster.

## Marketing ↔ Sales handoff

| Stage at marketing  | Handoff signal                           | Sales picks up                         |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Visitor             | Clicks /pilot CTA                        | Ali receives email, replies within 24h |
| Inbound application | Application form filled                  | Discovery call scheduled within 7 days |
| Content reader      | DM Samia / Abdout asking a real question | Ali briefed, follow-up within 48h      |
| Conference contact  | Business card / LinkedIn add             | Ali sends LinkedIn DM within 24h       |
| Case-study reader   | Downloads PDF                            | Email follow-up after 7 days           |

Anything the marketing surface produces is sales' job within 48 hours, or the inbound trust signal decays.
