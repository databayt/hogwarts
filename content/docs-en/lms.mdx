Stream is the platform's learning module. Students browse the shared [catalog](/docs/catalog), enroll in courses, and watch lessons; teachers and admins contribute videos that flow through a review pipeline and stay under the contributor's control — including per-video paid unlocks. Two journeys meet at the lesson page, and this page documents both, then the code layout that implements them.

The acceptance script lives in [hogwarts#377](https://github.com/databayt/hogwarts/issues/377) — the same flows as walkable test checklists.

## How it flows

### The full journey

The **student journey** (browse → enroll → watch → complete) and the **contributor journey** (upload → review → live → owner control) connect where an approved video lands in a lesson.

  chart={`flowchart TD
    subgraph SJ["🧑‍🎓 Student journey"]
        A["🧑‍🎓 Student browses courses"] --> B{"Course free or paid?"}
        B -->|"Free"| C["Enroll instantly"]
        B -->|"Paid"| D["💳 Card checkout → webhook confirms"]
        C --> E["📺 Open a lesson"]
        D --> E
        E --> F{"Does the lesson have a working video?"}
        F -->|"Yes"| G["▶️ Watch — progress saved"]
        F -->|"No / broken"| H["🎬 Fallback story clip plays<br/>(no progress recorded)"]
        F -->|"Only a PAID video"| I["🔒 Unlock · price → buy → watch"]
        G --> J["✅ Auto-complete + quiz → gradebook"]
    end
    subgraph CJ["👩‍🏫 Contributor journey"]
        K["👩‍🏫 Teacher/Admin uploads a video<br/>(URL or file) + picks audience & price"] --> L["⏳ Pending review<br/>school admins notified"]
        L --> M{"Audience?"}
        M -->|"School / Private"| N["🏫 School admin approves"]
        M -->|"Public / Paid"| O["🌍 Platform team approves"]

        O --> P
        P --> Q["Owner keeps control:<br/>visibility · revoke · replace · delete"]
    end
    P -.-> F`}
/>

Three invariants hold everywhere:

1. **An unpaid-for video never has a playable link.** The lock icon is cosmetic; the real paywall is that the server emits no video URL until the purchase exists.
2. **The player is never an empty black box.** No video → the marketing story clip plays — but the fallback never replaces a paid video's lock screen, and never writes lesson progress.
3. **Schools never see each other's private data.** School-visibility videos stay inside their school; only content approved as Public/Paid — by the platform desk, not a school admin — crosses schools, always credited to the uploader.

### Discover & enroll

<Mermaid
  chart={`flowchart TD
    V["🧑‍🎓 Student opens /stream"] --> Cat["Browse course catalog<br/>(search + filters)"]
    Cat --> Det["Course detail — chapters & lessons"]
    Det --> Q{"Free or paid course?"}
    Q -->|"Free"| Free["Enroll — instant, no payment UI"]
    Q -->|"Paid"| Pay["💳 Stripe checkout"]
    Pay --> WH{"Payment confirmed<br/>(webhook)?"}
    WH -->|"Yes"| OK["Enrolled ✔ + success page + email"]
    WH -->|"Cancelled"| No["Cancel page — not enrolled"]
    Free --> Dash["Course appears in 'My learning' dashboard"]
    OK --> Dash`}
/>

The catalog shows only published subjects the school has selected; content a school hid via override never appears for its students. Free courses enroll in one click with no payment UI; paid courses activate only after the Stripe webhook confirms. Free-course lessons are open even without enrolling — paid-course lessons are blocked for non-enrolled students, including by direct link.

### Watch a lesson

<Mermaid
  chart={`flowchart TD
    L["📺 Student opens a lesson"] --> Has{"Approved videos?"}
    Has -->|"None"| FB["🎬 Story fallback clip plays<br/>no progress · no auto-complete"]
    Has -->|"Yes"| Pick["Highest-ranked / school-preferred video selected"]
    Pick --> Locked{"Is it paid & unpurchased?"}
    Locked -->|"Yes"| Lock["🔒 Unlock · price pill"]
    Locked -->|"No"| Play["▶️ Plays — resume from last position"]
    Play --> Err{"Source fails to load?"}
    Err -->|"Yes"| FB
    Err -->|"No"| Prog["💾 Progress saved as you watch"]
    Prog --> Endd["Video ends → lesson auto-completes<br/>→ Up-next countdown to next lesson"]
    Endd --> Quiz["📝 Practice quiz → score lands in gradebook"]`}
/>

A lesson with no videos — or one whose file is dead or undecodable — plays the story fallback clip instead of a black screen. Fallback playback records no progress and never auto-completes (the manual "Mark as Complete" still works). Real videos resume where the student left off, auto-complete on finish, and offer up-next. With multiple instructors, chips switch the player; the school's preferred instructor plays by default, and every video credits its uploader. The practice quiz writes into the school gradebook when the student belongs to a class for that subject.

### Contribute a video

<Mermaid
  chart={`flowchart TD
    T["👩‍🏫 Teacher / 👨‍💼 Admin"] --> Btn["'Propose a Video' button<br/>(Settings → Videos tab)"]
    Btn --> S1["1️⃣ Pick the lesson"]
    S1 --> S2["2️⃣ Add the video"]
    S2 --> Src{"Source?"}
    Src -->|"URL"| U["YouTube / Vimeo / direct link"]
    Src -->|"Upload"| F["📤 Choose file → quota checked up-front →<br/>progress bar → done<br/>(MP4/WebM/MOV/AVI, up to 5GB)"]
    U --> Aud["Audience: Public / School / Private<br/>Pricing: Free / Paid (+price & currency)"]
    F --> Aud
    Aud --> S3["3️⃣ Review & submit<br/>('your rights' card shown)"]
    S3 --> P["⏳ Pending review — school admins notified,<br/>visible in My Videos"]`}
/>

Teachers, admins, and developers contribute through the same three-step dialog. Direct uploads go straight to storage with a progress bar; wrong types, oversized files, and a full storage quota are refused before any bytes move, and an uploaded-but-unsubmitted file cleans itself up when the dialog is abandoned. Quota counts the file's real size on storage, not what the client claims. Every submission lands as Pending — no role skips review — and the school's other admins are notified.

### Review & publish

<Mermaid
  chart={`flowchart TD
    P["⏳ Pending video"] --> Where{"Audience?"}
    Where -->|"School / Private"| SA["🏫 School admin — Review tab"]
    Where -->|"Public / Paid"| PT["🌍 Platform team — /catalog/approvals<br/>(may tweak audience/price before approving)"]
    SA --> D1{"Decision"}
    PT --> D2{"Decision"}
    D1 -->|"Approve"| Live["🎉 Live in the lesson, uploader credited"]
    D1 -->|"Reject + reason"| Rej["Contributor notified, sees the reason"]
    D2 -->|"Approve"| Live
    D2 -->|"Reject + reason"| Rej
    Live --> N["🔔 Contributor notified"]`}
/>

Two desks. The school admin's Review tab approves content that stays inside the school; anything Public or Paid belongs to the platform catalog desk, because it surfaces in every school — a school admin sees Approve disabled with an explanation (and the server refuses regardless), while Reject stays available. Both desks notify the contributor, and a rejection carries its reason into My Videos.

### Ownership, control & paid unlocks

<Mermaid
  chart={`flowchart TD
    subgraph OC["Owner controls — My Videos ⚙️"]
        O["👩‍🏫 Owner"] --> Vis["Change visibility any time"]
        Vis --> Wide{"Widening an APPROVED video to Public?"}
        Wide -->|"Yes"| Rev2["Goes back to Pending —<br/>platform re-review"]
        Wide -->|"No"| Now["Applies instantly"]
        O --> Rvk["🛡️ Revoke — instantly Private + CDN purge"]
        O --> Rep["♻️ Replace file — back to Pending"]
        O --> Del["🗑️ Delete — gone + quota released"]
        O --> Pw["Remove paywall (explicit, warns: no refunds)"]
    end
    subgraph BP["Buying a paid video"]
        S["🧑‍🎓 Student sees 🔒 Unlock · price"] --> CO["💳 Card checkout"]
        CO --> WH["Webhook confirms"]
        WH --> Un["Video unlocked for that student<br/>(time-limited signed links)"]
    end`}
/>

The contributor owns the video for good: visibility changes apply instantly, except widening an already-approved video to Public, which resubmits it for platform review (narrowing is always free). Revoke flips to Private and purges the CDN; replace resets approval; delete releases the storage quota. A paid video can't be un-paywalled through the visibility menu — only the explicit remove-paywall action, which warns that buyers aren't refunded. On the buying side, a lone paid video shows an Unlock-with-price pill on the lesson hero, checkout runs through Stripe, and the unlock is per-student with time-limited signed links.

### Behind the scenes

<Mermaid
  chart={`flowchart LR
    Set["⚙️ Settings — overview · enrollments ·<br/>instructors · review · videos"] --> Shape["Shape the whole feature"]
    Pref["🎚️ Instructor preference (per subject)"] --> Order["Which video plays first, school-wide"]
    Ovr["🙈 Content override"] --> Hide["Hide a chapter/lesson/video<br/>for this school only"]
    Quota["📦 Storage quota"] --> Cap["Caps direct uploads per school —<br/>checked up-front, counted from real bytes"]
    Par["👪 Parent view"] --> Kid["Child's progress, read-only"]
    Mail["📧 Resend emails"] --> Ev["Enrollment + completion emails"]`}
/>

Settings tabs are role-gated (teachers see only Videos; admins see all five, with a pending-count badge on Review). Instructor preference sets the default video source per subject school-wide; content overrides hide catalog items for one school without affecting others; parents get a read-only view of their child's progress; enrollment and completion emails go out via Resend. The whole surface — player, dialogs, review desk, search — follows the school's language, Arabic RTL included.

## Structure

The block follows the [mirror pattern](/docs/architecture): routes under `app/[lang]/s/[subdomain]/(school-dashboard)/stream/` are thin wrappers, and everything lives in `src/components/stream/`. Read fetchers in `data/catalog/` are server-only modules wrapped in React `cache()` — deliberately **not** `"use server"` actions.
