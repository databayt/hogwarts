## 30-Day Survival Plan

### Week 1: Connect + Research (Days 1-7)

- [ ] Email 249Startups (ahmed@249startups.com, hiba@249startups.com)
- [ ] Follow 249Startups on LinkedIn, engage with posts
- [ ] Research 20 private schools in MENA (use directories below)
- [ ] Join LinkedIn groups for school administrators
- [ ] Create simple demo video (Loom, 2 min, Arabic + English)
- [ ] Apply: Mastercard Foundation EdTech Fellowship

### Week 2: Outreach (Days 8-14)

- [ ] Send 20 cold emails/LinkedIn messages to schools
- [ ] Follow up on 249Startups response
- [ ] Post on LinkedIn: "Building school management for MENA"
- [ ] Apply: Orange Corners Sudan (if open)
- [ ] Join Sudanese diaspora Facebook groups as backup channel

### Week 3: First Pilot (Days 15-21)

- [ ] Get 1 school (ANY MENA school) to agree to pilot
- [ ] Remote onboarding call (Zoom/WhatsApp video)
- [ ] Setup their school in system
- [ ] Collect first feedback
- [ ] Apply: EdVentures Egypt, WISE Accelerator

### Week 4: Validate + Revenue Path (Days 22-30)

- [ ] Get video testimonial from pilot school
- [ ] Use testimonial to reach 5 more schools
- [ ] Discuss pricing: "$50-100/month" or "$0.50/student"
- [ ] Connect with co-founder/partner from 249Startups network
- [ ] Submit 3 more accelerator applications

## Emergency Backup Plans

**If no response after 2 weeks:**

- Offer to build custom features for 1 school in exchange for paid contract
- Pivot to consulting: "I'll digitize your school for $500" (one-time setup)
- Freelance on Upwork/Mostaql while continuing outreach

**If need immediate income:**

- Freelance development (your skills are valuable)
- Offer Next.js/React training to developers
- Sell school management setup as service (not SaaS)
